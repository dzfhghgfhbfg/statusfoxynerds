About us

Foxy Nerds is a worldwide Social Influence marketplace powered by AI that connects the most ambitious brands with the most creative influencers Brands are the most powerful storytellers of our time. But most of them have more and more difficulties to reach their targeted audience. The way people consume media has changed for good. For the first time people are going to spend more time on internet than in front of TV. Brands are aware of this, and spend a lot of money advertising on digital channels. But, with the raise of ad blockers, people have now the choice to see advertisements, creating a huge challenge for brands communication. The loss for publishers is estimated to be, in the best case, near 16 billions of dollars for 2020. At Foxy Nerds, we are convinced that social influence is an answer to this challenge. People, and more especially millennials, trust people. To reach people, a brand needs to find the right ambassador sharing the same values. These ambassadors publish authentic contents to their audience with a high rate of engagement, and can be the voice of the brands. 30% of consumers are more likely to buy a product recommended by a non-celebrity blogger. By the way Foxy Nerds Studio is also a human journey started in 2017 by 2 brothers Nicolas and Frederic Bruneau. We’re headquartered in Lille, more precisely in Euratechnologies, and our team is growing up quickly. To learn more about Foxy Nerds Studio, visit www.foxynerds.studio and to plan a demo with our team contact us at contact@foxynerds.studio.
